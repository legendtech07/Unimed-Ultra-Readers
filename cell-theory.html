Study Material

Review the key concepts before taking the quiz:

Cell Theory

  • All plants and animals are composed of cells
  • All cells come from preexisting cells
  • Cells are the smallest living units
  • Each cell works to maintain itself
  • Homeostasis results from coordinated cell activities

Cell Division

  • Mitosis: produces identical daughter cells
  • Meiosis: produces sperm or egg cells
  • Cell cycle phases: Interphase and M Phase
  • Mitosis stages: Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase
