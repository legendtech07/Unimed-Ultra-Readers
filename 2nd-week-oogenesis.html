Presentation Summary

This quiz is based on the lecture presentation "Second Week of Development: Bilaminar Germ Disc" by Dr. Uwejigho Raphael E.

Topics covered include:

  • Day 8: Blastocyst implantation and differentiation
  • Day 9: Lacunar stage and primitive yolk sac formation
  • Days 11-12: Uteroplacental circulation and extraembryonic mesoderm
  • Day 13: Primary villi and definitive yolk sac formation
  • Clinical correlates: Ectopic pregnancy and implantation failure
