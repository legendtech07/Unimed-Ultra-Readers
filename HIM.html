Course Overview

This course covers fundamental concepts in Health Information Science including:

  • Definition of health information science
  • Distinction between data, information, knowledge and wisdom
  • Attributes of health information
  • Relationship of information science to other disciplines
  • Information commodity as an economic good
  • Various information theories and models
  • Health information processing methods
  • Data operations in automated health information management systems
